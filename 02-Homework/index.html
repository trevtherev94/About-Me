About Me


I was born and raised in the rural areas of Northern Michigan. After falling in love with the mountains during a visit, I moved to Denver in 2017.

I make a living in Denver as an Arborist, doing what I can to take care of our beautiful trees. In my free time I enjoy hiking, camping, reading, and anything to do with music!
